Plastic moving totes are heavy duty plastic storage boxes with hinged lids.Some people call it plastic moving bins. They are storage and distribution boxes that are designed to stack when full and nest when empty, saving up to 75% valuable storage space.
These Attached lid storage containers are commonly used for many types of applications, such as office moves, distribution, logistics, events and industrial storage. Large crates are commonly used in the removal industry.Our range of plastic crates are available in high density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP).

Features

  • Lid interlocks to stay closed
  • Eyelets on each end allow secure closure with zip ties
  • Containers can be stacked when lid is closed or nested when lid is open
